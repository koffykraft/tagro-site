Choosing The Right STIHL

Not every machine suits every farm

A smallholder maintaining a home garden may require a different machine from a pepper farmer clearing estate paths or a contractor working daily.

The right choice depends on the work: cutting diameter, vegetation type, daily running time, operator experience, service access and spare availability.

If you are unsure which model is suitable, contact a TAGRO branch and describe your work. We will help recommend an appropriate machine.
