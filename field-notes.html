Why Field Notes

Useful lessons from regular work

Not every useful lesson comes from a manual.

Some come from a machine that failed after the first rains. Some come from a blocked filter. Some come from a pepper garden that behaved differently from the one next door.

Field Notes collects practical observations from TAGRO branches, customer visits, workshops and farms across Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
