Why Guides

Useful information before problems begin

Good equipment lasts longer when used correctly. Irrigation systems perform better when maintained properly.

TAGRO Guides brings together practical information from field experience, workshops, customer visits and manufacturer recommendations.

These guides are intended to help equipment owners, farmers, estate managers and irrigation users get better results from their investments.
