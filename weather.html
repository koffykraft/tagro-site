Why Weather Matters

Field conditions change faster than calendars

Rainfall, humidity, dry spells and seasonal transitions affect crops, irrigation systems and equipment performance.

This page collects practical weather-related observations from TAGRO branches, customer visits and field experience.

The aim is not to provide forecasts, but to provide useful context for decisions made in farms, plantations and home gardens.
