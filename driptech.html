Why dripperless

Maintenance

Fewer emitter parts

No separate emitters means fewer small parts in the line.

Pressure

Runs on less

Designed for low-pressure operation in suitable layouts.

Practicality

Simple field use

Useful where low-cost, straightforward irrigation is more important than a complex system.
