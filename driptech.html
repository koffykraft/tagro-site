Why dripperless

Clogging

Fewer points of failure

No separate emitters means fewer parts that can block or wear out.

Pressure

Runs on less

Operates at pressures as low as 0.2 kg/cm², removing the need for a high-powered pump.

Cost

Lower to install and run

Simpler construction keeps both the upfront and running cost down compared to traditional drip systems.
