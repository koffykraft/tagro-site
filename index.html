About TAGRO

Run from the field, not only from the counter.

TAGRO is a family-run agricultural business operating across Kerala and Tamil Nadu. We supply outdoor power equipment, irrigation systems, spare parts and service support through our branch network, while continuing to work with farms and plantations ourselves.

That practical experience informs what we stock, what we recommend and how we support customers.
