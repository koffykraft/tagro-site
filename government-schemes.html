Why This Page

Keep documents ready before applying

Scheme availability, eligibility and procedures can change by department, district and season.

This page will collect useful reminders for farmers and equipment users who are checking agricultural schemes, irrigation subsidies and farm support programmes.

Final eligibility, approval and procedure should always be confirmed with the concerned government department or authorised office.

Application Checklists

A general checklist to prepare before checking scheme eligibility.

  • Identity ProofAadhaar, voter ID or other accepted ID
  • Land DetailsTax receipt, survey number or ownership documents where required
  • Bank DetailsAccount details and passbook copy where required
  • Crop DetailsCrop type, area, spacing and planting information
  • PhotosField photos may help during enquiry or documentation
  • Official ConfirmationAlways confirm eligibility with the concerned department
