Why This Page

Learning from our own land

TAGRO is not only a counter for equipment and irrigation products. We also work with land, crops, water, weather and machines ourselves.

This page will collect observations from our own fields, estate work, crop trials, irrigation use and machinery experience.

The notes here may begin small, but over time they can become one of the most useful parts of the TAGRO site.
