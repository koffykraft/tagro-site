Why Cordless

A cleaner way to handle regular clearing work

Cordless brushcutters are useful where lower noise, easier starting and reduced fuel handling matter.

They are especially suitable for home gardens, institutions, resorts, hospitals, schools, small farms and regular maintenance work.

For heavy estate clearing and long daily use, petrol brushcutters may still be the better choice. TAGRO can help compare both options.

Before Buying

Questions To Ask

A cordless tool should match the work, not only the product catalogue.

  • Work areaHow much area must be cleared in one session?
  • VegetationGrass, weeds, creepers or heavier brush?
  • RuntimeWill one battery be enough or should a second battery be planned?
  • UsersWill it be used by staff, family, contractors or maintenance workers?
  • Noise sensitivityIs the work near homes, guests, schools, hospitals or offices?
