Why Troubleshooting

Small checks before a service visit

Many equipment and irrigation issues have simple causes that can be identified quickly in the field.

This library collects practical observations from TAGRO workshops, service counters and field visits.

The goal is not to replace service, but to help users identify common problems and know when professional attention is required.
